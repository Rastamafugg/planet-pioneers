## What it is

Strategic tips for playing M.U.L.E. well. Primary value for this project is shaping the **computer-player AI** — the strategies described here are what AI should exhibit (or deliberately not exhibit at easier difficulties).

## When to read

When working on [game/ai.md](../game/ai.md), particularly:

- Opening-round land pick priority (mountain plots for Smithore? food plots for management time?).
- Mid-game tradeoffs (produce-to-sell vs hoard-for-shortages).
- Auction strategy — when to buy, when to sell, collusion behavior in Tournament.
- Food-first principle (food = management time).
